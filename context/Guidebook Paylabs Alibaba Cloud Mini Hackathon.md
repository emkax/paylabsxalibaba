# Paylabs Mini Hackathon 2026: Redefining Digital Payments

**A collaboration between Paylabs, Alibaba Cloud Indonesia, and BINUS University**.

## 🚀 Program Overview

The **Mini Hackathon 2026** is a one-time workshop and innovation competition designed to solve real-world digital payment industry challenges. The program integrates industry problems from Paylabs with Alibaba Cloud's AI and cloud technology, powered by the innovation of BINUS students.

* 
**Goal:** Participants must identify ecosystem problems and develop a **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** that is applicable and implementable.


* 
**Focus:** Problem-solving based on actual industry challenges rather than just theoretical ideas.



### Objectives & Benefits

* **Objectives:**
* Encourage AI-based digital financial solutions.


* Build an inclusive and sustainable national fintech ecosystem.


* Increase technological and financial literacy among the youth.


* Identify and nurture future AI and technology talent.




* **Benefits for Participants:**
* Direct exposure to the fintech industry.


* Learning opportunities from Paylabs and Alibaba Cloud practitioners.


* Mentoring from BINUS academics.


* Certificate of program completion and a real-world portfolio project.


* Opportunities to win cash prizes.

## 📋 Participant Requirements

* 
**Eligibility:** Active BINUS University students (D3/D4/Bachelor/Master) with a valid ID.


* 
**Team Size:** Individuals or teams of up to **4 members**.


* 
**Technical Setup:** Participants must register for an Alibaba Cloud account and have a credit card for registration.


* 
**Project Scope:** Solutions must be innovative digital finance products integrated with **AI and Cloud infrastructure**.


* 
**Deliverables:** Final submission must be an MVP (Mobile app, website, chatbot, etc.).


* 
**Originality:** Submissions must be original works that have not won previous awards.



---

## 💡 Relevant Case Studies & Applications

Participants are encouraged to explore topics related to "AI + FinTech". Key areas include:

* 
**Smart Financial Assistant:** AI chatbots for personal finance management and spending analysis.


* 
**Suspicious Transaction Detection:** Machine learning systems to identify fraud or unusual activities.


* 
**Financial Sentiment Analysis:** Monitoring news/social media for economic trends and public sentiment.


* 
**AI Customer Support:** Training bots with transaction data to assist with refunds and failed transactions.


* 
**Smart Financial Visualization:** Interactive dashboards for cash flow predictions and spending charts.


* 
**AI Onboarding:** Streamlining merchant onboarding using AI.

## 📝 Submission & Proposal Rules

Proposal Structure 

1. 
**Cover & Team Introduction:** Team name, members, and NIM.


2. 
**Problem Statement:** Real industry/user impact.


3. 
**Insight & Opportunity:** Data-driven proof of market gaps.


4. 
**Proposed Solution:** Innovation and its impact on the Paylabs ecosystem.


5. 
**Technology/AI/Cloud Architecture:** Usage of AI and Cloud tech.


6. 
**User Journey / UX:** How users interact with the solution.


7. 
**Business Model:** Potential ecosystem usage within Paylabs and Alibaba Cloud.


8. 
**Impact & Feasibility:** Efficiency, revenue, and security benefits.


9. 
**Roadmap Implementation**.
